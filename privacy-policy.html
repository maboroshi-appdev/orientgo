Privacy Policy for OrientGo

Last Updated: February 2, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to OrientGo. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our app handles information.

2. Information We Collect

OrientGo does NOT collect any personal information.

Our app:

  • Does not require user registration or login
  • Does not collect names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Does not track your location
  • Does not access your contacts, photos, or other personal data
  • Does not use analytics or tracking tools

3. Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device, including:

  • Your bookmarked phrases
  • Your font size preferences
  • Expanded/collapsed section states

This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

4. Third-Party Services

OrientGo does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

5. Children's Privacy

Our app is safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
